Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rotate individual pages differently?
Yes! You can select specific pages and rotate each group independently. For example, rotate pages 1–3 by 90° clockwise and pages 5–7 by 180°, all before clicking Apply once.
Will rotation reduce the PDF quality?
No. This tool uses pdf-lib to apply PDF-native rotation metadata, meaning the actual content is not re-rendered or re-compressed. The original quality and file data are preserved perfectly.
Is my PDF uploaded to any server?
Absolutely not. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device. This makes the tool completely safe for confidential, legal, or personal documents.
What's the difference between CW and CCW rotation?
CW (clockwise) rotates the page 90° to the right — useful for landscape pages that appear sideways. CCW (counter-clockwise) rotates 90° to the left. 180° flips the page upside down.
How many pages can I rotate at once?
There is no page limit. You can rotate all pages in large PDFs at once. The speed depends on your device's browser performance, but for most PDFs it completes in under a second.

Rotate PDF Pages Online — Complete Guide

Scanned documents, camera shots of paperwork, and imported files often end up with pages in the wrong orientation. Our free PDF rotation tool lets you fix this instantly — rotate individual pages or the entire document by 90°, 180°, or 270°.

💡 Rotate all pages at once

To flip a landscape document to portrait, select all pages and rotate once. One click handles the entire document.

💡 Fix single pages

Scanned documents sometimes have one or two pages sideways. Select only those pages and rotate them individually.

💡 180° for upside-down pages

If a page is completely upside down, use the 180° rotation option to flip it correctly without affecting other pages.

💡 Save immediately

After rotating, download the corrected PDF right away. Refreshing the page will reset your changes.

Page rotation in PDF is stored as a metadata attribute called the page rotation matrix. Our tool modifies this matrix directly rather than re-rendering the page, which means text remains fully searchable and selectable after rotation. Vector graphics and embedded fonts are preserved exactly as in the original. The resulting file size is virtually identical to the original.
