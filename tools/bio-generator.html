Frequently Asked Questions

What should a good Instagram bio include?
A great Instagram bio has: who you are (name/brand), what you do (value proposition), a personality touch (emoji or hook), and a CTA (link in bio, DM open, etc.). Keep it under 150 characters and make every word count.
How is a LinkedIn bio different from Instagram?
LinkedIn bios are professional summaries highlighting your skills, experience, and career goals. Use keywords relevant to your industry for better discoverability. LinkedIn allows 220 characters in the headline and 2,600 in the "About" section.
Should I use emojis in my bio?
For Instagram and TikTok — yes, emojis add personality and break up text visually. For LinkedIn — use sparingly (1-2 max). For Twitter/X — emojis in the bio are fine. They help express your personality quickly within character limits.
How often should I update my bio?
Update your bio when your role, focus, or goals change. For personal brands, refresh quarterly. Always update the CTA (link in bio) when running a campaign or promotion. Fresh bios signal an active, engaged account.
