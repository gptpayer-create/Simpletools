Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a watermark on the downloaded meme?
No watermark at all. The downloaded file contains only your image and your text — nothing else. We don't add any branding, logo, or attribution.
Can I add more than two text layers?
Yes — there's no limit. Click "Add Text Layer" or "Add Custom" as many times as you need. Each layer is independently styled and positioned.
How do I move text on the canvas?
Just click and drag any text label directly on the canvas. The text follows your cursor in real time. You can also use the Position Preset buttons (Top, Middle, Bottom) in each layer's settings panel for quick alignment.
Does my image get saved or shared?
No. Everything runs entirely in your browser. Your image is never uploaded, transmitted, or stored — it stays on your device at all times.
