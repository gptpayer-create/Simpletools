Frequently Asked Questions

Is it legal to download YouTube videos?
Downloading for personal offline viewing is generally accepted. However distributing or using content commercially may violate copyright. Only download content you own or have permission to use.
What quality formats are available?
MP3 up to 320kbps (best audio), WAV lossless, MP4 up to 1080p Full HD, and WEBM. Quality depends on the original YouTube video — if uploaded in HD, HD download is available.
Why does the download open in a new tab on iPhone?
Safari on iOS opens media files instead of saving them. Long-press the Download button → "Download Linked File". Or use the Share icon to save. On Android tap and hold the download button → "Download link".
What if the auto-download doesn't work?
If auto servers are busy, the tool instantly shows 6 manual service options (Cobalt, SSYouTube, Y2mate, SaveFrom, etc.) — each one will work with one click. The manual fallback ALWAYS works.
Do YouTube Shorts and Music links work?
Yes! YouTube Shorts (youtube.com/shorts/...), youtu.be short links, and YouTube Music links are all supported. Live streams cannot be downloaded until they end.
