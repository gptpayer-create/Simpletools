Frequently Asked Questions

Can I blur multiple faces in one image?
Yes! Draw as many zones as you need before clicking Apply. All zones are processed in one operation with the same blur style and intensity.
Which blur style should I use?
Pixelate is the most recognizable and common on social media. Mosaic adds color variation. Gaussian is subtle and smooth. Black Bar is absolute — completely hides the area.
Can the blurred area be reversed?
Once downloaded, the blur cannot be reversed — that's intentional for privacy. You can reset the tool and start fresh if you need to redo the zones.
Is this GDPR compliant for sharing images?
Face blurring is one method used for privacy compliance, but legal compliance depends on your specific use case and jurisdiction. Consult a legal professional for guidance.
