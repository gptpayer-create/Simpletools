Frequently Asked Questions

Does paraphrasing avoid plagiarism?
Paraphrasing changes the wording while keeping the meaning. It reduces plagiarism risk but proper attribution is still recommended for academic work. Always cite your original sources.
What's the difference between Simplify and Shorten?
Simplify rewrites text using easier vocabulary and shorter sentences at the same length. Shorten actively condenses the text, removing details to make it more concise.
Can I rewrite the rewritten text again?
Yes! Click "Use as Input" to move the output back to the input box, then paraphrase again for further refinement.
