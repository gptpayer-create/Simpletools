Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legally binding?
Electronic signatures created with our tool constitute a valid eSignature under laws like the US ESIGN Act and EU eIDAS regulation for most standard agreements. For high-security legal contracts, a certified eSignature service may be required.
Is my document uploaded to a server?
No. All signing happens entirely in your browser using pdf-lib. Your document is never uploaded or transmitted to any server.
Can I sign on mobile?
Yes! The drawing pad supports touch input, making it easy to sign with your finger on tablets and smartphones.
Can I add multiple signatures?
Yes. You can place as many signatures as needed on any page. Each placement is tracked individually and can be deleted if needed.
