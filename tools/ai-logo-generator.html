Can I use these logos for my business?

Are the logos unique?
Each generation uses a unique random seed, so every logo is different. Regenerating gives you a fresh interpretation of the same concept.
Can I get a vector (SVG) version?
Currently we provide PNG and JPG formats. For vector formats, you can use tools like Adobe Illustrator's Image Trace or free alternatives like Inkscape to convert your downloaded PNG.
