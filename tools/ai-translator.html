Frequently Asked Questions

How is this better than Google Translate?
Our AI (Claude) understands context, idioms, and cultural nuances rather than doing word-for-word substitution. It produces more natural, fluent translations especially for complex or ambiguous text.
What is Back-Translate?
Back-translation takes the translated text and translates it back to the original language. This is a useful quality check — if the back-translation closely matches the original, the translation is likely accurate.
Can it translate documents?
Yes, you can paste text from any document. For best results with large documents, split them into sections of around 2,000 words each.
What does "Translate to 5 Languages" do?
This feature simultaneously translates your text into 5 popular languages (Spanish, French, German, Japanese, and Arabic) so you can quickly compare translations or prepare multilingual content.
