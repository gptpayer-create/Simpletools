Frequently Asked Questions

What is a favicon?
A favicon is the small icon displayed in browser tabs, bookmarks, and address bars.
What image formats are supported?
PNG, JPG, SVG, and WebP images are all supported as source files.
Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free. No signup, no watermarks.
What sizes should I generate?
At minimum, 16x16 and 32x32. Also add 180x180 for Apple touch icons.
How do I add a favicon to my website?
Place favicon.ico in your root directory. Add the link tag to your HTML head.

Favicon Generator — Complete Guide

This free online favicon generator tool is designed to work entirely in your browser — no uploads, no waiting, no software installation needed. It's fast, private, and completely free to use without any account or subscription.

💡 Use simple designs

Favicons are tiny — use simple, bold shapes or a single letter. Complex logos become unrecognizable.

💡 Square source image

Start with a perfectly square (1:1 ratio) image for best results.

💡 Test in browser

After adding your favicon, hard-refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R) to see the new icon in the tab.

💡 SVG favicons

Modern browsers support SVG favicons which scale perfectly at any size.
