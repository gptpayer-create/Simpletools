Frequently Asked Questions

What if my PDF has no fillable fields?
Use Click-to-Fill mode — click anywhere on the PDF to add a text box at that position. It works on any PDF, including flat (scanned) ones.
Can I check checkboxes?
Yes. Detected checkboxes appear in the left panel and can be toggled. In click-to-fill mode, you can also type ✓ or ✗ at any location.
Is my form data private?
Yes — completely. All processing happens in your browser using pdf-lib. No data is ever sent to a server.
Does it support digital signatures?
For digital signatures, use our dedicated Sign PDF tool which supports drawing, typing, and uploading signature images.
