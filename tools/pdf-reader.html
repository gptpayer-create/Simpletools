Frequently Asked Questions
Does the PDF get uploaded to a server?
No. The entire PDF is rendered using PDF.js entirely within your browser. The file never leaves your device.
Can I view password-protected PDFs?
If the PDF has a user password, you will be prompted to enter it. Owner-level restrictions on printing or copying are shown in the info strip.
How does the text search work?
The viewer extracts text from each rendered page and searches across all of them. Matches are highlighted in yellow, with the current match in orange, and you can navigate between them.
What PDF versions are supported?
PDF.js supports PDF 1.0 through PDF 2.0, including compressed object streams, Type 1, TrueType and OpenType fonts, JPEG2000, JBIG2, and most modern PDF features.
Can I extract all text from a PDF?
Yes — click the 📋 Extract Text button in the toolbar to open a modal with all text content from the document, which you can copy to your clipboard.
