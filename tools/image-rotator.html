Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image rotator free?
Yes, completely free. No signup, no watermarks, no limits.
Does rotating reduce image quality?
90°, 180°, and 270° rotations are lossless for PNG. JPEG rotations may involve minimal re-compression.
What image formats are supported?
JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP are all supported.
Is my image uploaded to a server?
No. All rotation is done locally in your browser using the Canvas API.
What is the difference between flip and rotate?
Rotate turns the image by a set angle; Flip mirrors it along an axis.
