Frequently Asked Questions

Which photos work best?
Portraits with clear subjects work beautifully for Cartoon and Anime. Landscapes suit Watercolor and Oil Paint. Sketch works on any photo with good contrast.
What's the difference between Cartoon and Anime?
Cartoon uses stronger edge outlines and higher color quantization for a Western animation look. Anime uses softer edges with more color levels and slight warm color boosting for a Japanese animation aesthetic.
Can I try multiple filters on the same image?
Yes! After applying a filter, click "Try Another" and select a different filter without re-uploading. Your original image is kept in memory.
Is my photo uploaded to any server?
No. All image processing is done using the Canvas API entirely in your browser. Your photos never leave your device.
