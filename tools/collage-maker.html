Frequently Asked Questions

How many photos can I add to a collage?
The number depends on the layout you choose. Layouts range from 1 photo (single full-bleed) up to 9 photos (3×3 mosaic). Select a layout first and the correct number of upload slots will appear automatically.
What resolution should I use for printing?
For standard 4×6 inch prints at 300 DPI, you need at least 1200×1800 pixels. Use the 2000px or 3000px canvas size option for high-quality print output. Make sure your input photos are also high resolution.
Can I use different aspect ratio photos?
Yes — each photo is automatically cropped to fill its cell in the layout using center-crop. This ensures a clean, consistent grid regardless of your photos' original aspect ratios.
Can I change the order of photos?
Click the × button on any slot to remove that image, then re-upload in the desired slot. Slots are numbered left-to-right, top-to-bottom in the layout order.
