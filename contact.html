Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files really never uploaded to a server?

Correct. All our tools process files entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. No data is ever sent to our servers. We technically could not access your files even if we wanted to.

Why is the tool not working on my device?

Our tools require a modern browser with JavaScript enabled. They work best on Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari (updated in the last two years). Very old browsers may not support the required JavaScript APIs. If you are having issues, try opening the page in a different browser.

Can I request a new tool or feature?

Absolutely — we love feature requests. Use the contact form above or email us directly. We prioritize features based on user demand, so if many people request the same thing, it goes to the top of our list.

Do you offer an API for developers?

We do not currently offer a public API. If you are interested in licensing our technology or discussing a technical partnership, please email us at hello@gptpayer.online with details about your use case.

How do you make money if the tools are free?

We display minimal, non-intrusive display advertising on our pages. We do not sell user data (we do not collect any), we do not have premium tiers, and we do not add watermarks. Advertising revenue covers our hosting and development costs.

Can I use GPTPayer.online tools for commercial purposes?

Yes. The tools are free for personal and commercial use. There are no restrictions on the type of files you process or what you do with the output — as long as you comply with our Terms of Service and applicable law.

My converted PDF looks different — why?

For image-to-PDF, output quality depends on source image quality. If blurry, the original image was low resolution. For Word-to-PDF, some complex formatting may not convert perfectly — our tool handles standard formatting well but may have limitations with advanced Word features like custom styles or embedded objects.

How many files can I convert at once?

No hard limit exists — practical limits depend on your device memory. Most phones and computers handle batches of 20–50 images without issues. For very large batches (100+ images), split into smaller groups for best performance.

Do the tools work offline?

Yes — once the page has fully loaded, the tools continue working even if your connection drops. All processing is local to your browser. No internet connection is needed after initial page load, making GPTPayer.online useful even in low-connectivity environments.

Is GPTPayer.online available in other languages?

The interface is currently in English, but the tools work with files in any language — Hindi, Arabic, Chinese, and any other script convert correctly because we process file data, not language content. We plan to add localized interfaces in future updates based on user demand.

Our Response Policy

We aim to respond to all messages within 24–48 hours on weekdays. Bug reports that affect core tool functionality are treated as high priority and typically addressed within 72 hours. Feature requests are reviewed and added to our development backlog — we send a confirmation when a requested feature ships.

We do not respond to spam, automated messages, or requests that violate our Terms of Service. For partnership and advertising inquiries, please provide specific details about your organization and proposal — vague inquiries may not receive a response.
